Data Visualization & Google Trends on Different Measures from Turkey¶

Introduction¶

In this assignment, three different data measures from the EVDS system and two different related 'keywords' from Google Trends are selected to investigate the relationships among them. The three data measures from EVDS are monthly exchange rates from 2014 to the end of 2021, the monthly house price index from 2014 to the end of 2021, and the car price index from 2014 to the end of 2021. The representative keywords from Google Trends are the volume of price increase searches(zam) and the volume of inflation(enflasyon) searches on Google. To discover the underlying relationships and correlations of these datasets, line plots, boxplots, bar plots, and correlation plots are used. Some of the questions intended to be answered are given below:

  • Is there any relationship between exchange rates and house prices?
  • Is there any relationship between exchange rates and car prices?
  • Is the behavior of the change in the car prices similar to the change in the house prices?
  • Do the changes in exchange rates, car prices and house prices have an impact on what people search on Google?
  • Can we observe this impact stated above on the keywords 'zam'(price increase) and 'enflasyon'(inflation)?

Price Increase('Zam') Data¶

'Zam' keyword is choosen to observe the changes in the data above. Since the increase in USD/TRY exchange rates will cause an increase in almost every goods sold in Turkey. For instance, the price of every basic product in markets will increase. Moreover, The cost of electricity, natural gas, and water will increase and Turkish people tend to express this situation by calling 'Zam'. Moreover, the increase in the house prices and car prices might cause people to search 'Zam' keyword on the Google as well.

Conclusion¶

When the correlations between variables are investigated, it can be seen that there are massive correlations between USD/TRY exchange rates, house prices, and car prices. The correlations between these variables are significantly close to zero, which indicates a nearly perfect positive correlation. When it comes to the relationship of these measures with the search volume of 'zam', they all have reasonable correlation with the search volume. The correlations of exchange rates, housing prices and car prices are 0.54,0.5,and 0.52 respectively. By looking at the correlations, we can conclude that these measures have a direct effect on the searches of 'zam' on google by people. When the correlations with the search volume of 'enflasyon' keyword, the correlations are higher than the 'zam' keyword. The correlations of exchange rates, housing prices and car prices are 0.71,0.63,and 0.69 respectively. These correlation amounts are significant amounts and adequate to say that the searches done by people about 'enflasyon' on Google is influenced by the three measures stated above.
